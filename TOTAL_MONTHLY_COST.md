- **Cons:** Only accessible on your machine

#### **Option B: Render.com (Free Tier)**
- 750 hours/month free compute
- Auto-sleep after 15 min inactivity
- **Cost:** $0/month
- **Pros:** Free, accessible anywhere
- **Cons:** Cold starts (slow first request)

#### **Option C: Railway (Free Trial)**
- $5 free credit per month
- **Cost:** $0/month (with free credit)
- **Pros:** No cold starts, fast
- **Cons:** May need to pay after trial

#### **Option D: Fly.io (Free Tier)**
- 3 shared VMs free
- 160 GB bandwidth
- **Cost:** $0/month
- **Pros:** Free, no cold starts
- **Cons:** Limited resources

**Recommended:** Render.com or Fly.io free tier  
**Your Cost:** **$0/month**
